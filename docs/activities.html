Activities to try

Ten adventures that will leave you with stories to tell for years.

Destinations Solo Travel Travel Tips Activities to try

Life is short. The world is huge. Adventure is calling your name! Whether you're after a new thrill, a heart-pounding rush, or simply a fun way to make memories, these will leave you with stories to tell for years.

1

Hot air balloon ride

Float above the earth and take in stunning panoramic views — serene, surreal, and unforgettable.

2

Dog sledding

Race across snowy landscapes pulled by a team of enthusiastic huskies — exhilarating, with heartwarming moments alongside the dogs.

3

Slingshot car ride

A high-speed open-air ride that goes from 0 to 60 mph in mere seconds — pure adrenaline.

4

Swim with dolphins

A perfect blend of nature, relaxation, and a little bit of excitement.

5

Ziplining

Harness up and soar between platforms through forests and jungles, with jaw-dropping views below.

6

Parasailing

Get hoisted into the air strapped to a parachute for a peaceful, exhilarating bird's-eye perspective.

7

Cave exploring

Venture underground among shimmering stalactites and ancient formations — from Iceland's ice and lava caves to Penn's Cave in the U.S.

8

Bungee jumping

Step off the edge of a bridge and plummet toward the earth — the ultimate way to conquer fear.

9

Shark cage diving

Come face to face with these magnificent animals in their natural habitat, safe inside a protective cage.

10

Sandboarding

Carve down massive sand dunes on a specially designed board.
